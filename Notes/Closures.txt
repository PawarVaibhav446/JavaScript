What is Closure ?
=> "function bundled along with its lexical scope forms Closure".
=> "It has access to its parent lexical scope".

Note => "function when they are returned from another function they still remembers its lexical scope."
  
Uses of Closures:
1) Module Design Pattern
2) Currying
3) functions like once
4) Memoize
5) maintaning state in async world
6) setTimeouts
7) Iterators
